About

MILAGRO is the biggest inter-collegiate cultural event that is organized in UVCE. Every year, this event draws a huge crowd of students,during the course of two days of power packed activities and competitions.

This mammoth number is a testimony of the popularity of the fest that has promulgated across all the colleges in Bangalore and other states.

It has been a Copernican revolution in cultural merriment that has bewitched many people and making them for ask more.The fest has been a culmination of various cultural and creative events

EVENTS

AIR CRASH

Ever wanted to play your favourite character? What if I tell you,you could play it and use your wit to survive an aircrash? Did I get your adrenaline rushing yet?No? What if i tell you,you could fight for this parachute by dissing at other characters you never liked? Get ready to roast or get roasted on main stage of UVCE exclusively only in MILAGRO

THE VOICE

When words fail,music speaks. Come let your music speak at MILAGRO's THE VOICE,the singing competition to test your vocal abilities.
The first round is a face off round followed by a 2+1 minute performance by the qualified contestants. To all the singers out there get ready with your tracks because the first step towards winning the competition starts with a Audition!!

KRUKSHETRA

Great stories like the Mahabharata don't belong to just one culture,they belong to the world. MILAGRO presents a trilling and exhilarating treasure hunt based on the Mahabharata.
Put your best foot forward because the hunt is on!!
Venue:Backstage

MUN

The Unites Nations stand for the freedom and equality of all people,irrespective of race,religionor ideology. Come be part of this magnificent institution with MILAGRO.
Students get the opportunity to role-play as delegates of the United Nations Assembly in order to address a global issue The task of the delegates is to find solutions to the provided issue in the form of opening statements,debates and speeches
At the end of the conference, the resolution passed by the "Chair of Assembly" will subsume the best solutions.

Calling all the artists to grab your drawing tools and strut our challenge.
DOODLES offer you to sketch/paint within a time limit of one and half hours on MILAGRO theme"Superheroes"
